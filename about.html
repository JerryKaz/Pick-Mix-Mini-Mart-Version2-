About Us

Serving the Amedzofe community with trust and dedication

Our Story

Welcome to Pick Mix Mini Mart, your trusted neighborhood retail store nestled in the beautiful hills of Amedzofe, Ghana. For years, we have been proud to serve our local community with quality products and genuine care.

What started as a small family venture has grown into a cornerstone of our community. We understand the needs of our neighbors because we are part of this community too. Every day, we work hard to ensure that essential provisions, fresh food items, refreshing drinks, and household necessities are always available when you need them.

Our shop is more than just a place to buy goods—it's a meeting point, a trusted resource, and a testament to the spirit of Amedzofe. We believe in honest business practices, fair prices, and treating every customer like family.

Our Mission

To provide the Amedzofe community with accessible, quality products while maintaining the highest standards of customer service and community care. We strive to be your reliable partner for all your daily needs.

Community First

We are deeply rooted in Amedzofe and committed to serving our neighbors with integrity and respect.

Quality Products

Reliable Service

Consistent availability and prompt service are our promises to you, every single day.

Trust & Honesty

Meet the Owner

Pick Mix Mini Mart was founded by Mrs. Ekissi Stella Myron with a simple mission: to provide the Amedzofe community with reliable access to quality goods at fair prices.

We believe in building strong relationships with our customers by understanding their needs and serving them with honesty, care, and respect.

As a business, we are proud to be more than just a shop we are a trusted neighbor and a dependable part of everyday life in Amedzofe.
